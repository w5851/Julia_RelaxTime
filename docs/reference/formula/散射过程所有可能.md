## 表1 独立的夸克-夸克散射过程

| 出入射粒子 | t 道介子 | u 道介子 |
|------------|-----------|-----------|
| \(u u \rightarrow u u\) | \(\pi, \eta, \eta', \sigma_{\pi}, \sigma, \sigma'\) | \(\pi, \eta, \eta', \sigma_{\pi}, \sigma, \sigma'\) |
| \(s s \rightarrow s s\) | \(\eta, \eta', \sigma, \sigma'\) | \(\eta, \eta', \sigma, \sigma'\) |
| \(u d \rightarrow u d\) | \(\pi, \eta, \eta', \sigma_{\pi}, \sigma, \sigma'\) | \(\pi, \sigma_{\pi}\) |
| \(u s \rightarrow u s\) | \(\eta, \eta', \sigma, \sigma'\) | \(K, \sigma_K\) |

## 表2 独立的夸克-反夸克散射过程

| 出入射粒子 | t 道介子 | s 道介子 |
|------------|-----------|-----------|
| \(u \bar{d} \rightarrow u \bar{d}\) | \(\pi, \eta, \eta', \sigma_{\pi}, \sigma, \sigma'\) | \(\pi, \sigma_{\pi}\) |
| \(u \bar{s} \rightarrow u \bar{s}\) | \(\eta, \eta', \sigma, \sigma'\) | \(K, \sigma_K\) |
| \(u \bar{u} \rightarrow u \bar{u}\) | \(\pi, \eta, \eta', \sigma_{\pi}, \sigma, \sigma'\) | \(\pi, \eta, \eta', \sigma_{\pi}, \sigma, \sigma'\) |
| \(u \bar{u} \rightarrow d \bar{d}\) | \(\pi, \sigma_{\pi}\) | \(\pi, \eta, \eta', \sigma_{\pi}, \sigma, \sigma'\) |
| \(u \bar{u} \rightarrow s \bar{s}\) | \(K, \sigma_K\) | \(\eta, \eta', \sigma, \sigma'\) |
| \(s \bar{s} \rightarrow u \bar{u}\) | \(K, \sigma_K\) | \(\eta, \eta', \sigma, \sigma'\) |
| \(s \bar{s} \rightarrow s \bar{s}\) | \(\eta, \eta', \sigma, \sigma'\) | \(\eta, \eta', \sigma, \sigma'\) |
